The Best Movies According to Miranda

My top 3 favorite movies of all-time.

The Fault in Our Stars

This movie dives into the world of cancer through a modern, teenage lovestory. I love the characters, Hazel Grace Lancaster and Agustus Waters. It is a good movie to watch when you want a good cry.

Inception

This is my favorite action-thriller. It messes with your mind in the perfect way and it will leave you questioning reality. The cinemetography is also incredible.

Call Me By Your Name

Movie set in the south of France. The love story is one of a kind and it is very visually appealing.
